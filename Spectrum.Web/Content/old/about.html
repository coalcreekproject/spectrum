About Us

The Company

SpectrumEM is setting the new standard for Emergency Management Information System (EMIS) functionality, usability, and collaboration. It was born out of a vision to provide full-spectrum Emergency Management software solutions that support all phases of Emergency Management – Planning, Preparation, Response and Recovery.

As the field is being revolutionized and professionalized, SpectrumEM will bring the latest in technology innovations to enable that transformation. Being based in Boulder, Colorado we are literally at the center of the software start-up explosion and have the ability to identify, integrate and implement these innovations before they hit the market. Therefore, by partnering with usour customers will achievehigher performance leading to a clear return on investment and significantly improved outcomes.

Our software solutions comprise scalable, elastic and secure infrastructure on a flexible platform. They are“cloud-native” or designed from the ground up to leverage the benefits of cloud technologydelivering superior speed and performance. Other EMIS options typically rely on legacy systems, are adapted from applications designed for other disciplines or are hosted in-house requiring the infrastructure, network and support staff that many government and other organizations don’t have.

Some providers may say they are cloud-based, but too often the applications are ported to the web after-the-fact and were not designed to fully leverage the power of the cloud. This can cause the app to be slow and unresponsive, sometimes even worse than when in the data center, which is frustrating at best and dangerous at worst. Cloud-native applications, such as SpectrumEM solutions, can be as much as 70% more efficient lowering your overall costs.

Another SpectrumEM differentiator is thatour team and systems and have a Process Orientation in order to ensure that the application isn’t static and only a repository for information, but rather, a tool to guide and support the business processes that you and your EM partners need to perform effectively and efficiently. In combination with our expertise in Organizational Development, we can increase your team effectiveness and inter-agency collaboration so that you are ready to perform when needed the most. Like a tennis player in the finals at Wimbledon, you and your teams have to be at your best when under the most pressure.


The Partners

Krista L. Welch - Founding and Managing Partner

Krista has 20 years of leadership and consulting experience in IT, Organizational Development &Effectiveness and HR. She developed her expertise working in Fortune 500 companies such as The Walt Disney Company, The Clorox Company and Sun Microsystems, as well as state and federal government. Past public sector roles have included serving as Chief of Staff to the Secretary of Technology and CIO at the Colorado Governor's Office of Information Technology, supply chain change management consulting for the USPS and workforce planning for GSA. During her tenure in these roles she created multiple leadership development programs, developed an HR Information System and a proprietary Workforce Impact tool for advanced workforce planning and analytics. Additionally, Krista has conducted change management, communications and training efforts for several large scale ERP implementations.

Krista holds an M.S. from the Claremont Graduate University, home of the Peter F. Drucker School of Management, and a B.S. from Arizona State University. She loves the Colorado lifestyle including cycling, yoga and family time.


Patrick Welch – Founding Partner and Principal Architect

Patrick’s has built his advanced technology and strategic business acumen throughout a 24-year career in the IT field. He launched his first entrepreneurial venture early in his 20’s and built a business providing multimedia technology for marketing and educational purposes. His other past roles have included hardware sales, service desk support and deep experience in data storage in Fortune 500 companies such as Sun Microsystems and FedEx, as well as StorageTek and Hitachi Data Systems.

Ultimately, Patrick was drawn to software development because he was fascinated with the idea of doing puzzles for the rest of his life. As a software developer, Patrick has served as a Department of Defense contractor providing GIS related services as well as procurement and logistics software for the healthcare industry.

This background has resulted in his ability to apply advanced analysis skills and experience in principles of solution design to define appropriate customer solutions, scope the effort and provide technical leadership to ensure successful execution and customer success.

Patrick has an Associate of Applied Science degree in Emergency Management and Planning. Outside of work, Patrick enjoys practicing Filipino martial arts, birding and spending time with his son Garrett.
